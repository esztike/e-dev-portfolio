Hello there, I’m Eszti – a digital experience designer, lifelong Star Wars fan, and Lego enthusiast. I believe that good design should feel like a well-timed lightsaber reveal: unexpected, elegant, and deeply satisfying. As a problem solver, I aim to create experiences that feel both functional and human.

For the past five years, I’ve been shaping digital experiences that feel intuitive and emotionally resonant. I’ve designed for enterprise SaaS platforms, nonprofit ecosystems, and even a honey brand inspired by sensory storytelling. No matter the project, I’m driven by clarity, connection, and the belief that thoughtful design can make complex systems feel beautifully simple. Collaboration, curiosity, and empathy are the constants in my work.

I believe that knowing how a product is built makes me a better designer, and knowing who it’s built for makes me a better developer. My transition into web development is driven by a desire to own the full lifecycle of a digital experience, bridging the gap between pixel-perfect design and functional, clean code.
